About Us

Finspree Solutions Limited is a consultancy company providing 360-degree solutions to businesses. We specialize in Turn Around of sick units by implementing business process re-engineering.

Our expertise lies in providing comprehensive solutions to businesses facing challenges due to lack of professionalism, absence of internal monitoring and control mechanism, substantial pilferages, higher attrition rate, cashflow crunch etc. We help them navigate complex situations and achieve sustainable growth.

We assist existing management in dealing with day-to-day business affairs effectively and efficiently. We help in managing suppliers and creditors to achieve higher profitability and support achieving higher sales turnover by coping with competitors.

We help our clients/business partners in hiring, training and effectively managing human capital — the most important aspect of business. We believe in nurturing talent through training and support.

We are also specialised in providing consultancy for project financing (debt and/or equity) and complete Banking Solutions to operating units.

Our core competence lies in transformation of businesses through structure formation and strengthening of key business aspects — moving from traditional to professional management.

Established in Kenya and expanding to Uganda, Tanzania and other neighbouring countries. Our senior management — a group of ex-bankers and professionals with more than three decades of aggregate experience in project finance, business banking, and investment advisory — drives our growth. Our goal is to become the first-choice financial advisor in Kenya, EAC, India and Globally.

Founded by Mr. Mohan Singh Shekhawat and Mr. Jabeer Basha Ellur Shaik — seasoned bankers and finance specialists with more than two decades of expertise each.
